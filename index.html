Forms

  • Form labels should be more more than 50 characters in length. If your question requires additional character put it in the help text.
  • Thou shall avoid placeholder text.
  • Thou shall only label optional fields. Required fields will not have indication they are required.
  • Validation should happen as the user tabs/clicks away from the field he/she were on.
  • Thou shall use a suggested terms dropdown when more than 10 choices. Semantic UI possibly(?)
